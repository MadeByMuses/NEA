# Civis

> By far the greatest and most admirable form of wisdom is that needed to plan and beautify cities and human communities.
> Socrates

Civis is a web-based project for AQA A-levels. The NEA's (Non-exam assessment) aim is to teach about urban development and understand the influence that urban development has.

## How to play?
It is as easy as going to [this link here](https://madebymuses.github.io/NEA/) and playing it for yourself!
